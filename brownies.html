easy brownies recipe

This easy brownie recipe takes about 30 minutes to make from start to finish for a chocolaty treat!

ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

stpes

  1. step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. step 2: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  3. step 3: Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  4. step 4: Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
